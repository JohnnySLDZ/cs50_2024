Imagine Dragons is a renowned American pop rock band formed in 2008 in Las Vegas, Nevada. The group is currently composed of Dan Reynolds (lead singer), Wayne Sermon (guitarist), and Ben McKee (bassist). Former member and drummer Daniel Platzman was an integral part of the band during much of their rise to fame, contributing to their signature sound. The band first gained widespread attention with the release of their single "It's Time". Their debut album, Night Visions (2012), launched them into international stardom, featuring hit singles like "Radioactive", celebrated for its unique fusion of genres, and "Demons". Over the years, Imagine Dragons have become a defining name in modern pop rock.
